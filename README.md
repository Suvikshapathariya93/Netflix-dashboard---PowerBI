# ðŸ’¼ Business Impact & Use Cases
This POWER BI dashboard provides meaningful insights that can support content teams, marketing teams and business strategists in making data-driven decisions. Key use cases include:

**1. Content Strategy & Acquisition**
- Identify which ratings and content types (TV-MA, TV-14, Movies vs TV Shows) dominate the platform.
- Helps Netflix teams decide what kind of content to acquire or producing more of.
- Insights into top countries can support regional content investments.

**2. Audience Targeting & Marketing**
- Rating distribution highlights which audience segments (kids, teens, adults) are most served.
- Supports targeted marketing campaigns for countries or content types showing rapid growth.

**3. Talent & Casting Analysis**
- The "Total Cast by Title" treemap uncovers frequently featured actors, helping identify:
  - Popular cast for collaborations
  - Trending talent across movies/TV shows

**4. Release Planning & Trend Forecasting**
- Release-year analysis reveals how content volume has changed over time.
- Helps in planning yearly content calendars and predicting audience demand trends.

**5. Country & Regional Insights**
- Global map visualization identifies key countries contributing the most content.
- Useful for expansion strategies, localization, dubbing investments and new partnerships.

**6. Competitive Benchmarking (Portfolio Health)**
- Understanding the balance between movies vs TV shows helps benchmark Netflix's portfolio against competitors.
- Reveals gaps in genre, country representation or ratings categories.
